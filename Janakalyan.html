"Shree Janakalyan Model Secondary School, Tharmare, Salyan"


Shree Janakalyan Model Secondary School in Tharmare, Salyan, Nepal, is a leading educational institution known for its modern facilities and dedicated teachers. It focuses on nurturing students academically, socially, and personally, fostering critical thinking and character development. Through extracurricular activities and community engagement, the school aims to empower students to become responsible citizens.
